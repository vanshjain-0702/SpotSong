HOW IT WORKS:

1. Images are displayed in the artist sidebar (50x50px circular)
2. When you search for an artist name, their full profile appears
3. Profile shows large circular image (150x150px) with all their songs
4. All songs link to YouTube with thumbnails extracted from video IDs
5. Click any song to see cover view with "Play on YouTube" button

Images are sourced from official social media (Instagram/Twitter) and hosted on Imgur for fast, reliable loading.
